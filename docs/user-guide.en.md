## Introduction

Mochi POS is an offline-first point-of-sale and business assistant app for market stalls, small shops, and light retail. It runs on iPhone, iPad, and Mac—ideal for sellers who want to get started quickly without dedicated POS hardware.

You can take orders and check out on your phone or tablet, browse order history, manage categories, variants, combos, and inventory-related data under **Products**, review past orders under **Orders**, and use analytics to understand your business. Data is stored on your device and synced and backed up across devices with **iCloud**.

The app supports 20+ languages and global currencies, multi-store switching, discounts and payment methods, flexible tax rules, and Bluetooth thermal receipt printing.

## Getting started

### First launch

On a fresh install, you’ll see the welcome screen:

![Welcome: choose currency and symbol position](doc_images/ipad_en_1.png)

Select a currency and where the symbol appears. When the amount preview looks correct, tap **Get started**. The app creates a store for you, and you’ll see sample products on the register right away.

### Taking orders

On the register, tap items to add them to the cart. If a product has options (e.g. ice level, sweetness), a picker appears first; after you choose, the item is added. The cart shows quantity, line totals, and a discount preview.

![Register and cart](doc_images/ipad_en_2.png)

Tap **Checkout** in the bottom-right to review the amount due, discount details, and add an order note. Pick a payment method, then confirm to complete the sale.

![Checkout and payment](doc_images/ipad_en_3.png)

### Orders

On **iPhone**, tap **Orders** in the bottom tab bar to open the orders screen. On **iPad** or **Mac**, tap **Orders** in the top bar. Orders are usually sorted newest first; tap one to see line items, totals, and status. Use the horizontal status filters (e.g. All, In progress, Ready for pickup, Completed, Cancelled) to narrow the list, or tap the filter button in the top-right for more criteria.

![Orders list and filters](doc_images/ipad_en_4.png)

### Analytics

On **iPhone**, open **Orders** from the bottom tab bar, then tap the **chart** icon in the top-right to open analytics. On **iPad** or **Mac**, tap **Statistics** in the top menu bar (Chinese UI shows「统计」). After you open it, switch between day, week, month, and year; tap the date row to jump to a single day or a custom range. You’ll see revenue, order count, trends, category breakdowns, sales reports, and daily sales. **Free** plans include today, yesterday, last 7 days: total sales, total orders, sales report, and daily sales. **Paid** plans unlock the full **17 metrics** and **11 charts**, plus period comparison.
