# Documentation

## Introduction

**Mochi POS** is an offline-first point-of-sale and business assistant for stall owners, small shops, and light retail. It runs on **iPhone**, **iPad**, and **Mac**—ideal if you want to get started quickly without dedicated checkout hardware.

On your phone or tablet you can take orders, check out, and browse order history. Use **Product management** for categories, variants, bundles, and inventory; open **Orders** for past receipts; and use **Statistics** for business insights. Data is stored on your device and can sync and back up across devices with **iCloud**.

The app supports **20+ languages** and global currencies, multi-store switching, discounts and payment methods, advanced tax rules, and **Bluetooth thermal receipt printing**.

This documentation is in progress and being refined. Last updated April 12, 2026.

If you have any questions, please email [support@tutoorapp.com](mailto:support@tutoorapp.com).

### Orders

Open **Orders** from the tab bar (on iPad / Mac it’s in the top bar). The list is usually newest-first; tap a row for details, amounts, and status. Use the horizontal **status filters** (e.g. All, In progress, Ready for pickup, Completed, Cancelled) to narrow the list, or tap the **filter** button in the corner for finer control.

### Business statistics

On **iPhone**: open **Orders** from the bottom tab bar, then tap the **chart icon** in the top-right corner to open Statistics.

On **iPad / Mac**: open **Statistics** from the top menu bar.

Once you’re in Statistics, switch between **day / week / month / year**, and tap the date row to jump to a single day or a custom range. You’ll see revenue, order count, trends, category breakdowns, sales reports, and daily sales.

**Free** users get four core metrics for **today**, **yesterday**, and the **last 7 days**: total sales, total orders, sales report, and daily sales. **Paid** users unlock the full **17 metrics** and **11 charts**, plus **period comparison**.

## Core features

### Add a product

On **iPhone**: tap **Product management** in the bottom tab bar to open the product screen. Then tap the **+** button in the top-right corner to add a **single product** or a **bundle** (bundles are available from app version **1.0.2** onward). Below we walk through adding a **single product**.

On **iPad / Mac**: tap **Product management** in the top bar, then tap the **+** button at the top-right of the product list on the left to add a **single product** or a **bundle** (from **1.0.2** onward). Below we walk through adding a **single product**.

On **iPhone**: open **Product management** from the bottom tab bar, then tap the product you want to change.

On **iPad / Mac**: open **Product management** from the top bar, then tap the product in the list on the left.

### Delete a product

On **iPhone**: open **Product management**, then **swipe left** on the product you want to remove. Tap **Delete product** to delete it. If you only want to **temporarily stop selling** an item, take it off the shelf from **Edit product** instead of deleting it.

On **iPad / Mac**: open **Product management**, then **swipe left** on a product in the left-hand list. Tap **Delete product**. To pause sales only, use **Edit product** instead of deleting.
