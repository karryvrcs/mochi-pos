# Documentation

## Introduction

**Mochi POS** is an offline-first point-of-sale and business assistant for stall owners, small shops, and light retail. It runs on **iPhone**, **iPad**, and **Mac**—ideal if you want to get started quickly without dedicated checkout hardware.

On your phone or tablet you can take orders, check out, and browse order history. Use **Product management** for categories, variants, combos, and inventory; open **Orders** for past receipts; and use **Statistics** for business insights. Data is stored on your device and can sync and back up across devices with **iCloud**.

The app supports **20+ languages** and global currencies, multi-store switching, discounts and payment methods, advanced tax rules, and **Bluetooth thermal receipt printing**.

On a fresh install you’ll see the welcome screen. As shown below, choose a **currency** and where the **symbol** appears. When the amount preview looks correct, tap **Get started**. The app creates a store for you, and you’ll see sample products on the register right away.

### Orders

Open **Orders** from the tab bar (on iPad / Mac it’s in the top bar). The list is usually newest-first; tap a row for details, amounts, and status. Use the horizontal **status filters** (e.g. All, In progress, Ready for pickup, Completed, Cancelled) to narrow the list, or tap the **filter** button in the corner for finer control.

### Business statistics

Open **Statistics** from the tab bar (top bar on iPad / Mac). Switch between **day / week / month / year**, and tap the date row to jump to a single day or a custom range. You’ll see revenue, order count, trends, category breakdowns, sales reports, and daily sales.

**Free** users get four core metrics for **today**, **yesterday**, and the **last 7 days**: total sales, total orders, sales report, and daily sales. **Paid** users unlock the full **17 metrics** and **11 charts**, plus **period comparison**.
