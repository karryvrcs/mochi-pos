常见问题

FAQ

必须联网才能收银吗？ Do I need the internet to check out?

不需要。核心收银、计价、找零与促销计算均可在完全离线下完成，适合网络不稳定或户外的摊位与门店。

No. Core checkout, pricing, change, and promotions work fully offline—ideal for stalls and weak connectivity.

我的数据存储在哪里？会丢失吗？ Where is my data stored? Could I lose it?

数据默认保存在本机。建议开启 iCloud 备份以便换机或多设备同步；仍请定期导出 CSV 作为额外备份，尤其在重要大促前。

Data stays on your device by default. Enable iCloud backup for new devices and sync; exporting CSV regularly adds an extra safety net.

支持哪些小票打印机？ Which receipt printers are supported?

支持通过蓝牙连接的常见热敏小票机（具体型号以 App 内说明与实测为准）。小票版式可在应用内自定义。

Common Bluetooth thermal receipt printers are supported (see in-app guidance). Layouts are customizable inside the app.

可以在 iPhone 和 iPad 上同时使用吗？ Can I use it on both iPhone and iPad?

可以。搭配同一 Apple ID 的 iCloud 能力，可在多设备间同步与备份（以系统与 App 实际选项为准）。

Yes. With the same Apple ID, iCloud can sync and back up across devices (per system and in-app settings).

促销规则复杂时，价格算错怎么办？ What if promotions make pricing confusing?

结账界面会按您配置的规则自动汇总优惠；建议在正式营业前用测试订单走一遍流程，并善用导出记录与报表核对。

The cart applies your configured rules automatically. Run test orders before going live, then verify with exports and reports.
